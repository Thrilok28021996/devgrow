How does the rating work?

DevGrow uses SM-2 spaced repetition — the same algorithm behind Anki. Each rating controls when you'll see the card again:

1 — BlackoutComplete blank. Card resets to 1 day.
2 — WrongRemembered something but got it wrong. Resets to 1 day.
3 — HardGot it with serious effort. Interval grows slowly.
4 — GoodCorrect with some effort. Interval multiplies normally.
5 — EasyInstant recall. Interval grows faster, EF increases.

The Ease Factor (EF) starts at 2.5 and adjusts after every review. A high EF means longer gaps between reviews. Cards with EF ≥ 2.5 and interval ≥ 21 days are considered mastered.
